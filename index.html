About Us

Rocky - Israeli Competition Team in Combat Sports

Open to All

Accessible to every competitor from any gym and any federation in Israel. We believe combat sports unite beyond organizational boundaries.

Professional

A professional and comfortable training framework for competitors, with personal guidance, fight analysis, and strategic planning for every competition.

Cutting Edge

Striving to be at the forefront of combat sports knowledge - a center for deep learning, support, and inspiration for fighters across Israel.
